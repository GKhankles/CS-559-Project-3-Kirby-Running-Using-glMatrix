Description: The following project contains Kirby, the video game character, running with his arms waving in the air.

Instructions:
-The first slider controls how open Kirby's mouth is. Opening his mouth causes air particles of various colors to appear. The more Kirby's mouth is open, the larger the air particles.
-The second slider controls how fast Kirby runs and swings his arms.
